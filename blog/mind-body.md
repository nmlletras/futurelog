# Mind and Body Connection

We often treat the mind and body as two separate entities. We go to the gym to train our physical bodies, and we read books or meditate to train our minds. But the reality is that they are so deeply interconnected that one cannot exist in optimal states without the other.

Every thought you have produces a biochemical reaction in the brain. The brain then releases chemical signals that are transmitted to the body, where they act as the messengers of the thought.
