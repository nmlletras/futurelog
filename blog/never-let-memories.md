# Towards the Horizon

Before long the searchlight discovered some distance away a schooner with all sails set, apparently the same vessel which had been noticed earlier in the evening.

The wind had fallen, and she was rolling gently in the swell. There was a sense of profound peace in the scene. The stars burned brightly above, and the reflection of the searchlight lay like a golden path upon the water.

*“The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams.”*
